about us

Founded by the visionary architect Amal Benny, A-Concept Architecture Studio is a leading firm in Wayanad, Kerala, dedicated to crafting innovative and sustainable architectural solutions. We believe in seamlessly blending modern design aesthetics with the rich cultural heritage of Kerala, creating spaces that are both beautiful and functional. Our team of passionate and experienced architects is committed to exceeding client expectations. We offer a comprehensive range of architectural services, including residential, commercial, and sustainable design, ensuring your vision is realized with meticulous planning and execution. We are dedicated to creating spaces that enhance lives, inspire communities, and leave a lasting legacy in Wayanad.
